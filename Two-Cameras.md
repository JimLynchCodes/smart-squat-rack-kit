## The Correct Setup

We use:

        FRONT CAMERA
             ↓

      knee tracking
      stance width
      symmetry
      valgus collapse

────────────────────────

         SIDE CAMERA
              ↓

        squat depth
        torso angle
        hip travel
        bar path

Those are two different biomechanical viewpoints.
